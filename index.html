About Kilele

Kilele Consultants was formed in 2018 arising from a need of Quality & Proffesional Consultation Services, Training and Capacity Building in the Northern and Eastern Region of Kenya.
Currently based in Meru, Kenya, the organization has spread its wings to Samburu, Isiolo, Garrisa, Mandera & Wajir Counties engaging in life Changing Projects, Poverty Eradication training Programmes and Community Impactful Research.

We understand that different actors are involved in programmes and projects. This is why we provide our custom made and quality services to a wide range of clients, including;

  • National & County Governments
  • Non-Governmental Organizations
  • Development Agencies
  • Civil Society
  • Philanthropists & Foundations
  • Private Sector
  • Community-based Organizations
  • Faith-based Organizations
